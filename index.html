About

Onaghise Emmanuella, also known as Ella, is a multifaceted creative professional from Nigeria. As a devoted Christian, Creative Director, Fashion Stylist, and content creator, Ella brings a unique blend of style, art, and spirituality to her work. With a strong background in content creation, brand development, and project management, Ella has collaborated with numerous brands, including Lifeofcyzar, Theuglyand, and Luxebypaula. Her expertise spans UI/UX design, 3D design, video editing, phone photography, creative direction, and fashion styling. Ella's passion for storytelling and creative expression has led her to create engaging content for her audience on TikTok and Instagram (@ella._f.o.e.t.i.a). She is dedicated to inspiring and uplifting others through her work.
